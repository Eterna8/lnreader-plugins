Sea Monster Stew

Dakku-san
Romance Supernatural
  • About
  • Releases | 69

Synopsis

An era of pirates where beasts wearing human masks have seized control of the sea.

 

“Starving and exhausted, the wife barely regained her strength after eating the meat stew her husband made for her. But as soon as she finished talking with him, she threw herself into the sea.”


“……Did she take her own life?”


“Perhaps. Now, why do you think she threw herself into the sea?”

 

Even in this age where barbarism and romance coexist, people are enthralled by riddles.

 

“The stew the husband fed his wife… was made from sea monster meat.”

 

“…….”


“She couldn’t forgive herself for being satisfied after eating such a filthy monster.”

 

In a world where sea monsters in human form are treated worse than beasts,


a pirate who despises sea monsters kidnaps a woman for revenge.

 

“There was a pirate who had a beloved lover. One day, the lover ate the stew the pirate gave them and left his side. Why do you think the lover left?”

 

“They must have realized the pirate didn’t truly love them.”

 

“How could they have known?”

 

The woman swallowed her answer: If he really loved them, he wouldn’t have fed them one of their own kind.

 

The pirate had no idea.


He didn’t know that the woman he kidnapped was one of the very sea monsters he so despised.
